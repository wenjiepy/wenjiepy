### 📄 Bio

My name is **Wenjie Deng ** and I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, co-supervised by **Prof. Jian Wu** and **Dr. Haochao Ying**. Before that, I got my B.Sc. degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University, under the guidance of **Prof. Sanping Zhou**.

My current research interests primarily focus on **Medical Artificial Intelligence (CV)**, **Embodied AI (Surgical Robotics)**, and **Laboratory Robotics**. Specifically, my work explores:

* **Medical Image Analysis & Computer Vision:** Advancing techniques for precise clinical tasks, including complex liver volumetry and 3D resection planning.
* **Surgical Robotics & Embodied AI:** Integrating deep learning and multi-modal models to enhance the perception, decision-making, and control of surgical robotic systems.
* **Generative AI in Medicine:** Applying diffusion models and multi-modal approaches to solve challenging clinical scenarios.
* **Laboratory Automation:** Developing intelligent and efficient robotic solutions for modern laboratory environments.

Feel free to contact me if your research lies within these or related areas!
